Stretching

List of test devices

This page demonstrates the player's Stretching options.

Default (uniform)

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Black bars should appear to the top and bottom of the poster image.
Play video While video is playing, black bars should appear to the top and bottom of the video.
Play video Video should play in-browser or in fullscreen mode at native resolution
Fullscreen toggle In fullscreen mode, the video's aspect ratio should remain the same.
Depending on screen resolution, black bars may change in size and position.

Default, bars on sides

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Thin black bars should appear to the left and right of the poster image.
Play video While video is playing, bars should remain on the sides of the video.
Play video Video should play in-browser or in fullscreen mode at native resolution
Fullscreen toggle In fullscreen mode, the video's aspect ratio should remain the same.
Depending on screen resolution, black bars may change in size and position.

Uniform - snap to sides

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Poster should stretch to the dimensions of the player.
Play video While video is playing, video should stretch to the dimensions of the player.
Play video Video should play in-browser or in fullscreen mode at native resolution.
There may be black bars.
Fullscreen toggle In fullscreen mode, the video's aspect ratio should remain the same.
Depending on screen resolution, black bars may change in size and position,
if the aspect ratio of the screen is close to the video's, the video should fit the screen.

Stretching = none

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Poster should be horizontally and vertically centered (480x204px).
Play video Video should be horizontally and vertically centered inside the player at 480x204px.
Play video Should be same as "uniform".
Fullscreen toggle In fullscreen mode, the size of the video file will not change.

Stretching = fill

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Poster image should zoom to fill the dimensions of the player, either cropping the sides or the top and bottom of the image.
Play video The video should be zoomed and cropped in the same manner as the poster image.
Play video Should be same as "uniform" (i.e. stretching will not apply to video playback on mobile devices).
Fullscreen toggle The video should be zoomed and cropped in the same manner as the poster image in fullscreen mode.

Stretching = exactfit

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Poster image position Poster image should be distorted to fit the entire player space.
Play video The video should be zoomed and cropped in the same manner as the poster image.
Play video Should be same as "uniform" (i.e. stretching will not apply to video playback on mobile devices).
Fullscreen toggle The video should appear with the same dimensions as the poster image in fullscreen mode.

Stretching Toggle

ActionExpected result(s)Works?
Begin playback Dock icon will appear, which will cycle through the stretching options when clicked. Cycle through at least 4 times.
Pause the player Cycle through stretching options with the player paused.
Go to fullscreen mode In fullscreen mode, begin playing. Then cycle through stretching options.
